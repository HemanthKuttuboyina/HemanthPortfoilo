Privacy Policy for Mobile Applications

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile applications (the "Apps") created by the developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience when using our Apps.

2. Data Collection and Use

Our Apps are designed with privacy in mind and do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to our servers.

  • Local Processing: Our Apps primarily operate locally on your device to provide their features and functionality.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs that monitor your behavior.
  • Device Features: Depending on the specific App, it may utilize standard device features. These processes occur locally on your device, and no personal data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not share, sell, or disclose any user data to third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Apps do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Given that no personal data is collected from any users, our Apps are generally safe for use by children.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Apps may rely on frameworks such as Expo and standard React Native libraries for their operation. While our Apps themselves do not collect data, we recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any underlying operating systems (iOS, Android) and platform providers.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact the developer.
